Ingredients

  • spaghetti

  • sauce

  • oil

  • ground beef

  • onion

  • minced garlic

Steps:

  1. Heat pan with oil

  2. Add onion and cook until slightly transparent

  3. Throw in minced garlic, cook for 2 minutes and then add ground beef

  4. While meat is cooking start a pot of water to boil

  5. Cook ground beef until brown

  6. Add sauce to meat and change stove temp to med-low

  7. When the water is boiling add in the dried spaghetti

  8. Cook for 7 minutes or until aldente

  9. Plate food

  10. Enjoy!
